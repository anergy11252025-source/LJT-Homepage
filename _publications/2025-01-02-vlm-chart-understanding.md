This paper investigates the perception bottleneck in Vision-Language Models (VLMs) when understanding charts and visual data representations.

**Key Contributions:**
- Identification of perception bottlenecks in VLM chart understanding
- Comprehensive analysis of chart understanding challenges
- Novel approaches to improve VLM performance on chart comprehension
