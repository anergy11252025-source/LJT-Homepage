This paper, published at EMNLP 2024, investigates the existence of a universal truthfulness hyperplane within Large Language Models (LLMs).

**Key Contributions:**
- Discovery of universal truthfulness hyperplane in LLMs
- Theoretical analysis of truthfulness representations
- Empirical validation across multiple model architectures
