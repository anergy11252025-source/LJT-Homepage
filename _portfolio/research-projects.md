My research focuses on advancing natural language processing and machine learning through several key areas:

## LLM Reasoning and Reinforcement Learning
Development of novel methods for improving logical reasoning capabilities in large language models through scalable data synthesis and reinforcement learning approaches.

## Hallucination in Vision-Language Models
Investigation of perception bottlenecks and hallucination mechanisms in VLMs, with focus on chart understanding and visual reasoning tasks.

## LLM Truthfulness and Interpretability
Discovery and analysis of internal mechanisms governing truthfulness in large language models, including the universal truthfulness hyperplane concept.

## Key Achievements
- First-author publications in top-tier venues (EMNLP, ICML, NeurIPS)
- Research experience at leading AI labs (MINIMAX, Tencent, Shanghai AI Lab)
- Development of open-source tools and datasets for the research community
