I am a first-year PhD candidate at the [HKUST NLP Group](https://nlp.hkust.edu.hk/), supervised by [Professor Junxian He](https://jxhe.github.io/). My research focuses on natural language processing and machine learning, with particular interests in:

- LLM Reasoning and Reinforcement Learning
- Hallucination in Vision-Language Models (VLM)
- LLM Truthfulness and Interpretability
