Hey there! I am **Junteng Liu (刘钧腾)**, a first-year Ph.D. student at [HKUST NLP Group](https://nlp.cse.ust.hk/), supervised by [Prof. Junxian He](https://jxh.me/).

Research Interests
======
My research focuses on **Natural Language Processing (NLP)** and **Machine Learning**. Specifically, I am interested in:
- **LLM Reasoning and Reinforcement Learning**: Enhancing the reasoning capabilities of Large Language Models.
- **Hallucination in Vision-Language Models (VLM)**: Understanding and mitigating hallucinations in VLMs.
- **LLM Truthfulness and Interpretability**: Exploring the inner mechanisms of LLMs related to truthfulness.
